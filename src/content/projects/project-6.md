**Project Overview:**
This project was created as the part of our Techathon-2025, a 24 hours hackathon organised by [Adani Institute of Digital Technology Management](https://www.aidtm.ac.in/) and [Thinkbiz Technology](https://thinkbiz.co.in/)

Check it out at https://roadsafe.vercel.app <br/>
And the source code at https://github.com/NotTheRightGuy/roadsafe

### Technologies Used:

- **Frontend:** Next.js (v14)
- **UI:** Shadcn & v0 and Tailwind CSS
- **Backend and database:** Supabase
